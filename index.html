WELCOME

Serenity. The state or quality of being serene, calm, or tranquil; sereneness.


Safi Shores is your refuge. It is where you come to find peace in your surroundings and disconnect from the demands of everyday life. Located on the beautiful northern shore of Lake Superior we look out onto a 150 foot private glacial-stone beach with smooth, colourful stones along the waterfront. To our left, you'll feel the cool flow of energy that comes from resting on the mouth of Montreal River. We are located along Highway 17 - one of Canada's top roadtrip routes - nestled between provincial parks like Pancake Bay and Bachawana Bay to our south, and the majestic Lake Superior Provincial Park to our north, just a 15 minute drive away. Welcome, to your serene escape - make it your own.

OUR WATER

Guests can enjoy our private glacial-stone beach that spans 150 feet of Lake Superior Shore. Walk along the stones, or take a boat out and try your hand at fishing. On a hot summer day, take a dip in the lake and then lay out on the warm, sun-kissed stones for a natural therapy you can't beat.

EXPLORE

Safi Shores is the closest roofed property to Lake Superior Provincial Park. Within minutes, drive north to find yourself exploring all of the beauty the park has to offer, or drive south and discover beaches like Batchewana Bay and Pancake Bay while enjoying one of Canada's top ten driving routes.
